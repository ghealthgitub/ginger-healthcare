UAE healthcare ranks among the world's best. Cleveland Clinic Abu Dhabi is an extension of the US Cleveland Clinic. Hospitals use the latest robotic surgery, AI diagnostics, and proton therapy. Quality is comparable to the US and Europe at lower costs.

Yes. UAE hospitals excel at complex oncology, cardiac, orthopaedic, and neurological cases. Many doctors are recruited from the US, UK, and Germany. Cleveland Clinic AD and Burjeel handle advanced procedures with world-class outcomes.
