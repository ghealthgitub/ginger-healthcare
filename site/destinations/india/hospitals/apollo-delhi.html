About the Hospital

Indraprastha Apollo Hospital, New Delhi

Indraprastha Apollo Hospital, New Delhi, is the flagship hospital of the Apollo Hospitals Group ‚Äî India's foremost integrated healthcare provider. Established in 1983, it was the first hospital in India to be accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI) and has been consistently re-accredited for over two decades.

Located in Sarita Vihar, South Delhi, the 710-bed multi-specialty tertiary care hospital is one of the most sought-after destinations for international patients. It treats patients from over 120 countries and has a dedicated International Patient Services division with multilingual coordinators, visa assistance, and concierge services.

Apollo Delhi is especially renowned for its cardiac sciences programme (performing 6,000+ cardiac procedures annually), advanced oncology centre (with robotic surgery, CyberKnife, and immunotherapy), liver and kidney transplant programmes, and comprehensive orthopaedic and neurosurgery departments.

FAQs About Apollo Hospitals Delhi

Where is Apollo Hospital Delhi located?

Indraprastha Apollo Hospital is located in Sarita Vihar, South Delhi, near the Jasola‚ÄìApollo metro station. It's approximately 15 km from Delhi Airport (IGI) ‚Äî about 30-45 minutes by car.

What is Apollo Delhi best known for?

Apollo Delhi is especially renowned for cardiac surgery (6,000+ procedures/year), oncology (CyberKnife, robotic surgery, immunotherapy), organ transplants (liver, kidney, heart), neurosurgery, and orthopedics (robotic joint replacement).

How much does treatment at Apollo Delhi cost?

Costs vary by procedure. Indicatively: CABG from $5,000, knee replacement from $5,500, liver transplant from $28,000, cancer treatment from $3,000. Ginger Healthcare provides free, detailed cost estimates based on your specific case.

Does Apollo Delhi have international patient services?

Yes. Apollo Delhi has one of India's largest international patient departments, treating patients from 120+ countries. They offer multilingual coordinators, visa assistance, airport transfers, and currency exchange. Ginger Healthcare adds an additional layer of end-to-end support.
