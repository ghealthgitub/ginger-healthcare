About This Treatment

CABG Surgery â€” All You Need to Know

Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG), commonly called bypass surgery, creates new pathways around blocked coronary arteries to restore blood flow to the heart. It is the most effective long-term treatment for patients with severe coronary artery disease, especially those with multiple blockages or left main disease.

India has become one of the world's most preferred destinations for CABG â€” offering the same surgical quality as top Western centres at 80â€“93% lower costs. Surgeons like Dr. Naresh Trehan (Medanta) and Dr. Rajesh Sharma (Apollo) have collectively performed tens of thousands of CABG procedures with outcomes matching the best global benchmarks.

Indian hospitals offer all variants: on-pump (traditional), off-pump (beating heart), minimally invasive direct coronary artery bypass (MIDCAB), and totally endoscopic bypass. Most centres use arterial grafts (LIMA, RIMA, radial artery) for superior long-term results.
