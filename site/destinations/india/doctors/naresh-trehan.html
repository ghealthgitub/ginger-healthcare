About Dr. Naresh Trehan

Dr. Naresh Trehan is India's most renowned cardiac surgeon and the founder of Medanta ‚Äî The Medicity, one of India's largest multi-super-speciality hospitals. With over 40 years of experience and more than 48,000 cardiac surgeries, he is recognised globally as a pioneer in cardiovascular and cardiothoracic surgery.

Before founding Medanta in 2009, Dr. Trehan served as the Executive Director and Chief Cardiac Surgeon at Escorts Heart Institute, New Delhi, which he built into one of Asia's top cardiac centres. He trained at NYU Medical Center in New York and practiced in the US for several years before returning to India.

Dr. Trehan is known for performing complex, high-risk surgeries including redo CABG, valve repairs, aortic root replacements, TAVR/TAVI procedures, and surgery on elderly and comorbid patients. He has been conferred the Padma Bhushan (2001) and Padma Shri (1991) by the Government of India for his contribution to medicine.
