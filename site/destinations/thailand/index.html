Common Questions

FAQs About Treatment in Thailand

Is Thailand safe for medical tourism?

Yes. Thailand has been a leading medical tourism destination for over 30 years. Bangkok hospitals like Bumrungrad treat over 1 million international patients yearly. The country is safe, welcoming, and has excellent infrastructure.

Why is Thailand so popular for medical tourism?

Thailand pioneered medical tourism in Asia. The combination of JCI-accredited hospitals, experienced doctors, affordable costs, warm hospitality, and a beautiful recovery environment makes it uniquely attractive for international patients.

Do I need a visa for Thailand?

Citizens of 60+ countries can enter Thailand visa-free for 30 days. For longer medical stays, a medical visa can be arranged. Ginger Healthcare assists with all visa documentation.

What\'s included in Thailand treatment packages?

Our Thailand packages include: doctor consultations, surgery/procedure, hospital stay, medications, pre/post-op tests, airport pickup, accommodation assistance, and a dedicated care manager. Many hospitals also offer wellness recovery packages.

How long do I need to stay in Thailand?

It depends on the procedure. Wellness checkups take 1‚Äì2 days. Cosmetic procedures need 7‚Äì14 days. Major surgeries like cardiac or orthopaedic require 14‚Äì21 days. Many patients extend their stay to recover at Thailand\'s beautiful resorts.
