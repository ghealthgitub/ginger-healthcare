Why Choose India for Medical Treatment?

India's appeal as a medical destination comes down to three factors: quality, cost, and accessibility. The country is home to some of the world's most experienced surgeons ‚Äî many of whom trained at institutions like Johns Hopkins, Mayo Clinic, and the Royal College of Surgeons ‚Äî yet the cost of treatment is a fraction of what you'd pay at home.

India has over 40 JCI-accredited hospitals (the gold standard for international healthcare quality), with cutting-edge technology including robotic surgery, proton therapy, and advanced genomic testing. English is widely spoken in major hospitals, and international patient departments are equipped to handle everything from visa assistance to interpreter services.

Visa & Travel Planning

International patients need a Medical Visa (not a tourist visa) to receive treatment in India. Here's what you need to know:

Documents Required

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Hospital invitation/appointment letter
  • Medical reports confirming the need for treatment
  • Proof of sufficient funds
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Completed visa application form

Processing Time

Medical visas typically take 3‚Äì7 working days to process. In urgent cases, expedited processing is available in most countries. Your attendants can apply for a Medical Attendant Visa simultaneously.
